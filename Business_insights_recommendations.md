# 📊 Business Insights & Recommendations
## Ecommerce Sales Analysis — Performance Report
**Period:** January 2023 – June 2025
**Dataset:** 1,200 Orders | 1,189 Unique Customers
**Tools:** Advanced Excel | SQL

---

## 🔍 Executive Summary
This analysis examines 2.5 years of ecommerce transaction data to uncover critical performance gaps and growth opportunities. Despite consistent order volumes across 30 months, the business faces significant revenue leakage 
through high cancellation and return rates, near-zero customer retention and untapped pipeline revenue. This report outlines six key findings and actionable recommendations to address these challenges.

---

## 📈 Key Findings

| # | Finding | Metric |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | High order loss rate | 41.4% of orders cancelled or returned |
| 2 | Near zero customer retention | Only 0.93% of customers returned |
| 3 | Instagram dominates acquisition | ₹2,75,285 revenue — highest channel |
| 4 | Chair is top revenue product | ₹1,95,620 total revenue |
| 5 | Large untapped pipeline | ₹2,56,328 in pending orders |
| 6 | Free shipping beats discounts | FREESHIP used 313 times vs SAVE10's 286 |

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1.Reduce Order Loss Rate
**Finding:** 20.8% cancellation rate and 20.6% return rate — combined 
41.4% order loss is severely impacting confirmed revenue.

**Recommendations:**
- Conduct customer exit surveys to identify top cancellation reasons
- Improve product descriptions and images to reduce return-driven disappointment
- Introduce a hassle-free exchange policy instead of returns to retain revenue
- Set up automated order confirmation and tracking notifications to reduce cancellations

**Expected Impact:** Even a 10% reduction in cancellation rate could recover approximately ₹48,000 in additional confirmed revenue.

---

### 2.Build a Customer Retention Strategy
**Finding:** Cohort analysis across 30 months reveals near-zero retention 
— almost every customer orders once and never returns. Only 11 out of 1,189 customers made a repeat purchase.

**Recommendations:**
- Launch a loyalty rewards program — points per purchase redeemable on next order
- Set up post-purchase email sequences at Day 7, Day 30 and Day 60
- Offer personalised product recommendations based on first purchase
- Create a referral program — reward existing customers for bringing new ones

**Expected Impact:** Improving retention from 0.93% to even 5% would significantly reduce customer acquisition costs and grow lifetime value.

---

### 3.Double Down on Instagram
**Finding:** Instagram drives the highest revenue at ₹2,75,285 across 259 orders — outperforming Email, Google, Facebook and Referral channels.

**Recommendations:**
- Increase paid social media budget allocation toward Instagram
- Run retargeting campaigns for website visitors via Instagram ads
- Partner with micro-influencers in the home and electronics space
- Create Instagram-exclusive discount codes to track conversion

**Expected Impact:** Scaling the top performing channel could proportionally increase overall revenue without increasing acquisition costs elsewhere.

---

### 4.Convert Pipeline Revenue
**Finding:** ₹2,56,328 sits in pending orders — representing 52% of confirmed revenue waiting to be unlocked.

**Recommendations:**
- Investigate why orders remain in pending status for extended periods
- Set up automated payment reminder notifications
- Introduce a 24-hour order confirmation window with automatic cancellation for unpaid orders
- Streamline checkout and payment processing to reduce drop-offs

**Expected Impact:** Converting even 50% of pipeline revenue would add ₹1,28,164 to confirmed revenue immediately.

---

### 5.Restructure Discount Strategy Around Free Shipping
**Finding:** FREESHIP coupon was used 313 times vs SAVE10's 286 uses 
— customers clearly respond better to free shipping than percentage discounts.

**Recommendations:**
- Make free shipping the primary promotional offer across all campaigns
- Set a minimum order value threshold for free shipping to protect margins
- Phase out percentage discount coupons or limit to seasonal promotions
- Test free shipping vs cashback offers to further optimise conversion

**Expected Impact:** Aligning discount strategy with customer preference improves conversion rates without unnecessarily reducing margins.

---

### 6.Introduce Product Bundling Strategy
**Finding:** Chair is the top revenue product at ₹1,95,620 and Desk ranks 6th — these are naturally complementary home office products.

**Recommendations:**
- Create a Home Office Bundle — Chair + Desk at a slight discount
- Cross-sell Laptop and Monitor as a Work From Home package
- Display complementary products on product pages to increase basket size
- Offer bundle-exclusive free shipping to incentivise larger orders

**Expected Impact:** Bundling complementary products can increase average order value from ₹407 to potentially ₹700-800 per transaction.
